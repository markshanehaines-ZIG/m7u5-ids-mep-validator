# One natural-language Employer's Information Requirements (EIR) clause per
# block. Blank lines separate clauses; lines starting with # are comments.

All air-handling units shall declare the supply-air design flow rate via
Pset_AirHandlingUnitTypeCommon.NominalAirFlowRate so the commissioning
team can verify against the room-by-room load schedule.

Every domestic-hot-water tank shall publish its capacity in litres on
Pset_TankTypeCommon.NominalCapacity and shall be assigned to an
IfcDistributionSystem of predefined type DOMESTICHOTWATER.

All chillers shall declare nominal cooling capacity in watts on
Pset_ChillerTypeCommon.NominalCapacity for the energy modelling team.
